# Genesis Roofing Ltd

## Project goal
Build a high-converting UK roofing company website for Genesis Roofing Ltd.

## Working rules
- Use UK English
- Do not invent accreditations, guarantees, years of experience, awards, locations, or reviews
- Use [TO BE PROVIDED] where business facts are missing
- Keep outputs practical, concise, and ready for Bolt
- Prefer a roofing-focused site over a general building site
- Do not convert every image folder into a public service page

## Asset rules
- Do not use low-resolution images as hero backgrounds
- Do not use mislabeled HEIC/HEIF files as WebP
- Treat missing hero imagery as a blocker, not something to guess around
- Prefer image choices already approved in the manifests
