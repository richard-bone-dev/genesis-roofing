# Bolt Style Refresh Prompt - Genesis Roofing Ltd

Use `https://erind-ontrack-market-2qhm.bolt.host/` as a UX/style reference only.

Apply its level of polish, spacing discipline, rounded media, soft card styling and page-to-page consistency to Genesis Roofing Ltd. Do not copy its branding, copy, colour treatment, imagery, icons, industry styling, testimonials, statistics or claims.

## Keep

- Current framework and routing.
- Current page structure.
- Current content, widgets, forms and CTAs.
- Light header.
- Strapline/top bar above the main header.
- Dark footer.
- Existing v1 page set and navigation.
- Existing image rules and approved image choices.
- `[TO BE PROVIDED]` placeholders for missing business facts.

## Update The Design System

- Headings and navigation: Manrope.
- Body copy, form fields and supporting text: Inter.
- Buttons, inputs, selects and textareas: `rounded-xl`.
- Cards, repeated widgets, quote blocks and small proof panels: `rounded-2xl`.
- Large panels, hero media, feature images, gallery media and CTA bands: `rounded-3xl`.
- Borders: softer, low-contrast neutral borders.
- Shadows: subtle and practical, used on cards/widgets/forms/media panels.
- Spacing: cleaner section padding, consistent vertical rhythm and less cramped card interiors.
- UI shape: less square, more polished, still trade-appropriate.

## Tone And Feel

- Premium.
- Trustworthy.
- Practical.
- Roofing/trade appropriate.
- Professional rather than playful.
- Not interior-design-led.

## Component Guidance

- Keep the header light and straightforward, with clear navigation and visible quote/call CTAs.
- Keep the top strapline slim, useful and restrained.
- Keep the footer dark and consistent across every page.
- Make service cards feel refined and robust: rounded image, soft border, subtle shadow, clear heading, concise copy and visible CTA.
- Make contact and quote widgets spacious, readable and easy to complete.
- Make gallery/proof panels polished without inventing project details.
- Keep trust sections factual and placeholder-led where proof is missing.

## Do Not

- Redesign the structure.
- Change framework.
- Convert the site to a SPA.
- Over-animate.
- Add fake reviews, ratings, awards, accreditations, guarantees, years of experience, service areas or locations.
- Turn conditional pages into live navigation.
- Use low-resolution images as hero backgrounds.
- Use unapproved homepage hero imagery.
- Copy the reference site literally.

## Final Check

Before finishing, check that every live v1 page uses the same typography, corner radii, border softness, card styling, header treatment and dark footer, while preserving Genesis Roofing Ltd's existing content and roofing focus.
